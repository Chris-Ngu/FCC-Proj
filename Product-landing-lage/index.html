Features

Easy to understand

We understand that a game of this caliber is quite complicated and hard to get into. Have no fear, we have simplified the version of Dungeons and Dragons that everyone loves and implimented our own twist. This way, everyone can join in on the fun without having to read an encyclopedia about the rules and lore!

Digital + Physical Copies

With every purchase of the game, we will include a digital version as well as a physical copy. The game is more portable than ever, anyone can play no matter what platform you choose. And the best part about this: you don't have to worry about licensing. Buy it once and own it forever, say goodbye to DRM!

Open-Source Nature

Have you ever wanted to improve a product that because some feature was missing or a feature wasn't to your liking? Have no fear, you can contribute to this project in any way, shape, or form. Sign up for our newsletter to find out when you can sign up for beta participation!
